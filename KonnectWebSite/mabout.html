COMPANY PROFILE


VISION :


To be a global leader in the IT industry that delivers high standard and quality solutions, products and services to achieve a greater impact for a safer future.


WHAT WE ARE :


KONNECT UNIVERSAL SOLUTIONS INCORPORATED is a domestic Information Technology (IT) company duly organized and existing under Philippine laws, primarily engaging in the construction and management of software solutions related to disaster response management. Our products provide monitoring solutions in cases of natural calamities such as earthquakes and typhoons, as well as man-made occurrences such as traffic accidents, flooding, crimes, property fire and other emergencies.
Our company also deals with the seismic monitoring of buildings to measure the soundness and integrity of the structure during and when earthquakes occur.


WHO WE ARE :


KONNECT UNIVERSAL SOLUTIONS INCORPORATED is composed of highly skilled and experienced full-time associates from various business sectors who are well-rounded in handling disaster management activities. Each member works hand-in-hand in committing to providing quality solutions thru our products and services that work together with technology-driven methodologies to ensure immediate and accurate real-time data gathering and recording to be used for quick response and decision-making during emergency responses.

KONNECT UNIVERSAL SOLUTIONS INCORORATED provides products and services that would enable our clients both from the private sector and LGUs to assist them in monitoring, responding and dispatching to any type of emergencies or disasters.

KONNECT UNIVERSAL SOLUTIONS INCORPORATED includes:
Bacoor City, Cavite - Command Centre Disaster Software Program
Guihulngan City, Negros Oriental - Analog Radio System
Vista Land, MDC-Ayala Inc. - Building Seismic Monitoring Systems
Passi City, Iloilo - Digital Radio System with Integrator and Repeater



Konnect Universal Solutions Incorporated is under a mother company called VERTICAL EQUIPMENT CORPORATION.



Vertical Equipment Corporation has been in existence since 2010 as a leading product service provider of various construction equipments such as tower cranes, gondolas, vertical elevators (hoists), scaffoldings, construction lifts and other various construction equipments to major construction companies in the Philippines.


Their major clients are: DMCI, Datem, MDC, EEI, Fressynet, Leighton Australia, Megawide and DDT to name a few.


Some of their big projects include J Centre Cebu, Ayala Mall, Emall Double Dragon Cebu, Perpetual Succour Hospital, Avida Cebu, Marco Polo Hotel, Globe Telecommunications Inc., Beacon Tower & San Lorenzo Towers in Makati as well as numerous small projects in Metro Manila.
